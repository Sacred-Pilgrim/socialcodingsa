Social Coding uses Computer Programming skills like Coding, to teach Maths and Science to scholars between the ages of 6-17 in low resource areas.

How We Work

We believe that to achieve the innovative goals we have set for ourselves as a country, young people nationally and across Africa—especially girls—must have the tools and skills to become change makers.

Therefore, in our programs, every student, regardless of where she is from, receives the schooling, mentorship, and leadership training in digital education such as coding and robotics.

Training

Learning digital skills such as Block Chain, A.I and innovative entrepreneurship about the world around them and earning the diploma/degree to prove it

Mentorship

Pairing the scholar with a facilitator who guides them through the course material and helps them build an MVP

Implementation

Helping the scholar use the skills they learns in real life academic situations and encouraging them to enter tech competitions that showcases their MVPs.
